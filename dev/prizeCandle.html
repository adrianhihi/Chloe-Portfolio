Present Surprises With Prize Candle
My Role: Visual Designer (Marketing), Branding
Time: 2016.1
The Romance
Prize Candle is an e-commerce startup based in Santa Monica, CA, selling high quality soy candles. Every candle contains jewelry surprises worth up to $5,000.

I worked as a visual design consultant in the marketing team, helping with their social media campaigns and leveraging the email marketing success.
Direct The Focus
Email subscription is one of the Prize Candle’s most important website traffic resource. Among so much competition, a well-designed email is crucial to to capture the attention of the reader right away. The design goal is to engage the customers and create email marketing campaigns to reach a massive audience.

In most times, there’s only one image used to represent the brand, one headline, one description and one call-to-action button. Elements should be considered carefully to order the information neatly while still giving it a creative and personal touch.

I enjoyed the process greatly by playing around with colors, various typefaces, simple layouts, appealing headlines, strong images, and fun GIFs. In the meanwhile, I summarized a couple of tips that I’ve discovered during the process.
