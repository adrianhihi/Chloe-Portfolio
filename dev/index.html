Discover your path to a great career

Information Technology Academy is an interactive education program that provides systematic training to help engineers grow their software development skills.

The program focuses on coding, algorithms, data structures, and system design.

Our industry experts teach practical material that focuses on designing and implementing industry products and systems, with an emphasis on programming skills.

Course Offering

ITA Advanced Programming

Our flagship program, ITA Advanced Programming, focuses on four main development areas: coding fundamentals, coding principles, design and architecture, and technical communication. It is the first course that we recommend all of our students take, as it is designed to build the foundation for a deep understanding of software engineering principles.

ITA Projects

Our technology-specific program, ITA Programming Projects gives students deep training in three areas: Android development, big data, and full stack software development. It is designed to give a more thorough understanding of these areas, and is meant for students that have already displayed a firm grasp of computer science fundamentals.

ITA Master Programming

Our most demanding program, ITA Master Programming, is designed for more experienced developers that are interested in working on more complex engineering principles. This elite course is individually designed for each student, focusing on their specific needs and providing a more intensive training on deeper, more challenging subject matter helping students prepare for a career at top-tier companies.
