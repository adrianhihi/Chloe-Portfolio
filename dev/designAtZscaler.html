Projects at Zscaler
Zscaler is providing a cloud-based information security service for enterprises. It’s used by companies like GE, Siemens, Johnson Controls and securely connects users to their applications, regardless of device, location, or network. During the past two years at Zscaler, I designed over 50 projects individually, including products for our core product Zscaler Internet Access, Zscaler.com company website, Zscaler apps and internal tools. Unfortunately, I can’t share details due to the privacy reasons. So for now, I summarized some brief notes about some milestone projects I completed.
