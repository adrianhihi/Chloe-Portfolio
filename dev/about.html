Our Story

IT Academy was founded by Rick Sun and Jing Zhao, two Computer Science Ph.D. graduates from the University of Southern California. While working for prestigious Silicon Valley companies, they realized there was a gap between the hiring needs that employers have and the skill sets that candidates have. They came up with an idea and a plan to help eliminate this gap, and IT Academy was started.

About the Company

Information Technology Academy is an interactive education program that provides systematic training to help engineers grow their software development skills. The program focuses on coding, algorithms, data structures, and system design.

Our industry experts teach practical material that focuses on designing and implementing industry products and systems, with an emphasis on programming skills.

  • Our Mission

    To bridge the skills gap between employee supply and employer demand.

  • Our Vision

    To empower each member of the workforce with the skills they need to fulfill their career dreams.

About the Instructors

Our instructors have worked as engineering leaders for various top-tier Silicon Valley companies including Google, Facebook, Hortonworks, Uber, LinkedIn, Box, and Twitter.

Their real-world experiences help students gain and develop the knowledge and skills needed to have a successful software engineering career.

In addition to highly-successful careers, our instructors have been educated at some of the world’s top universities, giving them a strong foundation to both understand and teach challenging technical concepts.
